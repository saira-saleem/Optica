About Optica

Got Bad Glasses? Get Good Glasses. Optica is a proud optical retailer providing outstanding customer service for over 40 years. With a large selection of top designer frames, contact lenses, caring staff and doctors who care about you, we hold ourselves to a higher standard when it comes to helping you find your perfect eyewear.

Optica is committed to providing quality, Pakistan-made workmanship at affordable prices. Offering even more value, our 45-Day Guarantee provides our customers with peace of mind

Our History as a Vision Care Leader

A lot has changed since we opened our first retail location and laboratory in Sterling Heights. And, as we’ve grown, our pledge to our customers has never wavered: provide outstanding eye care, quality products at reasonable prices while exceeding expectations.

Growth should only come from satisfied customers, and we’re proud to say our expansion and growth have never compromised our first-class service. It’s the continued commitment to our customers that has allowed us to maintain both our exceptional service and unwavering standards since 2000.
